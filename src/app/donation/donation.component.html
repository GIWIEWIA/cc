VDEG's 9th Volunteer Camp

Activity Details

VDEG's 9th Volunteer Camp is hosted at Phagae Primary School! This unique opportunity invites volunteers from all backgrounds to come together for a week of impactful community service and educational enrichment. Throughout the camp, volunteers will engage in a variety of activities designed to support the school's educational goals and enhance the learning experience for students. Activities will include interactive teaching sessions, creative arts and crafts workshops, and constructive playtime activities. Volunteers will have the chance to share their skills and knowledge with eager young learners, fostering a supportive environment that encourages curiosity and growth.
